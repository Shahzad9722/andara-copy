About ARDARA

Our Land,
Our Home

ARDARA... Saudi Arabia's homegrown developer of world-class lifestyle destinations. Our name is inspired from two Arabic words – Ard (meaning land) and Dar (meaning home). We create magical destinations that salute nature, culture, and celebrate life.
